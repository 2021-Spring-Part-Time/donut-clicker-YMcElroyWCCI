Fred the Baker Baking Company began in 1954 when twin brothers George and Fred Weasley created delicious baked goods from the basement of a grocery store which was located across the street from current location. After brother George developed an allergy to flour, Fred and his wife, Jane became the sole owners of Fred the Baker Baking Company. In 1957 Fred moved the bakery to its current location at 1 North State Street in Columbus, Ohio where the delicious baked goods have been enjoyed by the citizens of Columbus and its surrounding communities for over six decades.

In 1974, Fred Weasley's son, Fred Weasley II, began working at Fred the Baker Baking Company. Enjoying the work, he moved on to production and learned the craft of baking specialized in doughnuts. Fred Weasley later gave the son the opportunity to purchase the bakery when he retired. The son became the second owners of Fred the Baker Baking Company in January 1987.

After 50 years of ownership, Fred Weasley II has decided he is ready to retire and wants to keep his love of donuts alive as a game that uses button clicks to create virtual donuts for the world to enjoy.

The inspiration for Donut Maker Clicker was derived from Cookie Clicker game.

Cookie Clicker is a javascript game created by Orteil on August 2013. The purpose of the game was for fun and 100% free. The game allows user to bake cookies by clicking on a giant cookie. Everytime the cookie is clicked, the number of cookies will increase. Once there are enough cookies, upgrades to have other ingredients are available. It is an endless gamesplay that last an indefinite amount of time. Visit Cookie Clicker Wiki for more information.
